ABOUT US

Eurotunity is a service agency that helps Chinese students to broaden their horizons by studying in Europe. We facilitate your process of applying to UK and EU universities, support you in your academic and personal decisions, and help you relocate and integrate into your new home in Europe. Eurotunity offers a fast and transparent service, helping you find your ideal educational environment, so that you can enjoy an international academic experience that is tailored to your individual needs and preferences.
