Bachelor Programme

A Bachelor is a 3-4 year entry-level programme that you start right after high school. Taking a Bachelor programme outside of China does not only allow you to benefit from the prestige and quality of a Western education, but also gives you a first hand long-term insight into Western society, culture and business.

While going abroad directly after high school may be a personal challenge for some, it definitely lets you stand out from your peers! What more shows personal flexibility, dedication and an open mindset then leaving your friends and familiy behind to have an international experience at a renowned academic institution in Europe. With the extensive and continuous support from Eurotunity you will not only find an institution and a course that is tailored to your indiviudal needs and preferences, but you will also benefit from our longstanding experience in helping Chinese students to study abroad.

Masters Programme

If you already completed your Bachelor in China or elsewhere, then you could go abroad for 1-2 years for your Masters degree! Building up on the knowledge you gained during your Bachelor programme, a Masters programme abroad allows you to get a different perspective on your field of study, whilst giving a long-term insight into Western society culture, business and university life.

While you usually build up your academic knowledge during a Bachelor programme, the Masters allows you to either deep-dive into a topic that interests you specifically or to look at your field of study from a different angle. Completing your Masters abroad is a great way to get new insights, alternative perspectives and new views on your field of expertise. Eurotunity supports you in two ways: Firstly, our team looks at your individual case to understand and discuss with you which universities and programmes are most appropriate for your personal development. Secondly, we help you with all the formalities that you probably do not want to deal with: Visas, Flights, Relocation, Accommodation and much more. Here you will benefit from our great experience and exclusive deals that will allow you to save time and money without any stress!

Summer School

How about taking a Summer School at one of the world's most prestigious universities? Spending your summer in one of Europe's bustling cities allows you to not only expand your academic knowledge, but to also make a new, valuable experience and expand your understanding of Western culture. Honestly, is there a better way to spend your summer?

You would like to see more of the world, but don't want to commit to an entire degree outside of China? Maybe you have family to take care off or a partner that you don't want to be separated from. There can be many good reasons why you don't want to spend a long time abroad. However, that doesn't mean that you have to miss out from this experience altogether! There are many prestigous European universities that offer you to study and live on their campus for a shorter time period of about 1-3 months during the summer. While being shorter in time, these programmes still give you a first-hand experience of Western culture, whilst also allowing you to see your academic field from a different angle or to even, if you like, dive into in a completely different field!
